About Rob

Dedicated to Transforming Educational Outcomes

With extensive experience in education and leadership in behavior analysis, Rob Spain brings a unique combination of practical expertise and innovative thinking to school-based behavior analysis.

Professional Excellence

Board Certified Behavior Analyst since 2011 and recent International Behavior Analyst certification, representing over a decade of dedicated service in educational behavior analysis.

Innovation Leader

In 2020, Rob launched an innovative platform providing BCBA supervision tools and training for school-based professionals nationwide. This ecosystem supports BCBAs with resources for supervision, professional development, and evidence-based practice implementation.

Community Leadership

Currently serving as President of Behavior Analysts in Education SIG CalABA, leading strategic initiatives across California.

Projects

Professional Ventures

Rob Spain, BCBA, founder of Behavior School, has created innovative platforms and resources to support school-based behavior analysts nationwide. His work focuses on bridging the gap between evidence-based practice and practical implementation in educational settings.

Through comprehensive training programs and digital tools, Rob transforms how BCBAs approach their work in schools, reducing burnout while improving student outcomes.
