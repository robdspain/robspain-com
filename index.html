About Rob

Dedicated to Transforming Educational Outcomes

With extensive experience in education and leadership in behavior analysis, Rob Spain brings a unique combination of practical expertise and innovative thinking to school-based behavior analysis.

Rob Spain is best known as the “School BCBA,” redefining what it means for behavior analysts to work in public education. With over 25 years in schools, he has shown that BCBAs aren’t just crisis managers—they are systems leaders who can transform outcomes for students and staff across all tiers of support.

Rob proved this by creating Behavior School, an ecosystem of AI-powered tools, training programs, and professional networks designed to give school-based BCBAs and educators the structure they need to succeed. From Behavior Study Tools—an infinitely generating BCBA exam prep platform—to micro-apps for IEP goals, FBAs, and supervision, his innovations are already shaping daily practice in classrooms across the country.

As the President of the Behavior Analysts in Education SIG under CalABA, a district-wide Behavior Intervention Specialist, and adjunct faculty at Fresno Pacific University, Rob has led the integration of PBIS, Practical Functional Assessment, Skill-Based Treatment, and Acceptance and Commitment Training into real-world school systems.

After more than two decades of testing, refining, and scaling behavior systems, Rob continues to share his work through presentations, courses, and the Behavior School platform. His mission is clear: to equip every School BCBA to step into the role of systems leader—because schools run better when behavior analysts lead at every level.
