We’ve been told that if we just write better FBAs and more detailed IEP goals, the behavior will stop. That is a myth.

Individual support is Tier 3. But if your Tier 1 and Tier 2 systems are broken, Tier 3 will always be overwhelmed. You can’t "clinical" your way out of a "system" problem.
