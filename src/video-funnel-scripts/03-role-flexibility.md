**THE PROBLEM:** "Role Confusion." In our database, an "ACE Coordinator" and a "Board Member" have different permissions, but in the real world, those lines blur. If a user picks the wrong role during sign-up, they are currently locked out of the features they need, and the only fix is for an admin to delete their entire account and start over.
