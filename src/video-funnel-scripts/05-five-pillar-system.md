Here is the map. I call it the Behavior School Framework.

1. Audit the current chaos. 2. Design the Tier 1 safety net. 3. Train the staff (without boring them). 4. Implement with micro-coaching. 5. Data-driven refinement. 

This is how you stop being a fire-fighter.
